about climate change

Climate change is primarily caused by the increase of greenhouse gases due to human activities like burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes. This results in rising global temperatures, extreme weather events, melting ice caps, and rising sea levels, all of which threaten ecosystems, biodiversity, and human life. The effects of climate change also impact agriculture, water supplies, and health. To mitigate these impacts, it's crucial to reduce emissions.

To further combat climate change, individuals can also play a role by adopting sustainable practices like conserving energy, reducing waste, using public transport.
